Client: GreenSpace Landscaping

What product/service is being promoted?
GreenSpace Landscaping provides residential and small-commercial landscaping services, including lawn care, seasonal yard cleanups, mulching, garden bed refresh, basic planting, and small-area sod installation.

Who is the target audience?
• Homeowners ages 28–60 who want a clean, attractive yard but have limited time to maintain it themselves.
• Property managers and small business owners who need reliable and affordable outdoor maintenance services.

Which platforms are most suitable?
• Instagram – Ideal for sharing before-and-after photos, short transformation videos, and seasonal yard tips.
• Facebook – Best for local community engagement, customer reviews, promotions, and booking links.
• YouTube (optional) – Short educational videos showcasing lawn care tips and completed landscaping projects.

Proposed Hashtags:
#GreenSpaceLandscaping
#LawnCare
#GardenDesign
#LandscapingServices
#BeforeAndAfter

Promotional Strategy (2–3 sentences):
GreenSpace Landscaping will use Instagram to post weekly before-and-after transformations and quick seasonal tips to visually demonstrate service quality. Facebook will focus on local community engagement, seasonal promotions, and customer reviews to build trust. All posts will include a clear call-to-action directing users to the website to request a free quote.
